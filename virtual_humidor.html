The Th3rd Rule.

Gray Hogan’s personal cigar, blended by Karen Berger — the “Queen of Cigars.” Bold, complex, and built to stand out in any humidor. You can only get it here.

Wrapper
AJ Fernandez Cameroon
Binder
Nicaraguan Oscuro
Filler
Double Ligero · Estelí, NIC
Formats
Singles & Bundles

Named after The Tobacco Center’s 3rd Lounge Rule, this is not a house cigar that exists to fill a shelf. It’s the result of a real collaboration between Navy Veteran Gray Hogan and Karen Berger at her factory in Estelí, Nicaragua. The cigar is headlined by a rare AJ Fernandez Cameroon wrapper — beneath that, a rich Nicaraguan Oscuro binder and a potent double ligero filler from the legendary growing region of Estelí. This is not for the timid.
